# Aura Divine - Brand Colors & Theme

## Recommended Palette

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Primary | Soft Lavender | `#E6E6FA` | Headers, buttons, accents |
| Secondary | Blush Pink | `#FFE4E9` | Backgrounds, cards |
| Accent | Divine Gold | `#D4AF37` | CTAs, highlights |
| Background | Ethereal White | `#FDFBF9` | Page background |
| Text | Soft Charcoal | `#4A4A4A` | Body text |

### Extended Colors

| Color | Hex | Use |
|-------|-----|-----|
| Deep Lavender | `#9B89B3` | Hover states, footer |
| Rose Quartz | `#F7CAC9` | Testimonials |
| Champagne Gold | `#F7E7CE` | Subtle gold backgrounds |

---

## Alternative Options

### Option A: Healing Cafe Style (Minimalist)
| Color | Hex |
|-------|-----|
| Black | `#000000` |
| White | `#FFFFFF` |
| Gray | `#555555` |

*Pros:* Clean, premium, timeless
*Cons:* Doesn't evoke "angelic" or "divine"

### Option B: Aatmbodh Style (Corporate)
| Color | Hex |
|-------|-----|
| Blue | `#046BD2` |
| White | `#FFFFFF` |
| Dark Slate | `#1E293B` |

*Pros:* Professional, trustworthy
*Cons:* Too clinical for spiritual healing

### Option C: Recommended (Angelic) ✓
Lavender + Blush + Gold

*Pros:* Matches "Aura Divine" brand, spiritual, unique
*Cons:* None - aligns with client's stated preferences

## Logo Direction

**Style:** Elegant, minimal, ethereal

**Elements to consider:**
- Abstract angel wings
- Golden halo/arc
- Lotus flower
- Healing hands

**Colors for logo:**
- Main: Lavender `#E6E6FA` or Gold `#D4AF37`
- Text: Deep Lavender `#9B89B3`
