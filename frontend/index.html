FAQ

Your questions,
answered honestly.

Energy healing is often misunderstood. These answers are designed to give you clarity, not convince you.

Do I need to believe in energy healing for it to work?

No. Scepticism is welcome. Energy responds to intention and openness, not to intellectual belief. Many of the most transformed clients arrived as sceptics. All that's needed is genuine curiosity and a willingness to feel.

What does a session actually feel like?

Most clients describe warmth, tingling, a profound sense of calm, or spontaneous emotional release. Some see colours or imagery. Others simply feel deeply rested. Every experience is valid and unique to your energy field in that moment.

Can distance healing really work?

Yes. Energy is not bound by physical proximity — this is a foundational principle of both Reiki and quantum physics. Distance sessions are equally powerful to in-person work. Clients across India and globally report the same depth of experience.

How many sessions will I need?

A single session can create real, noticeable shifts. For deeper or long-standing patterns — trauma, chronic anxiety, significant life transitions — a three- or six-session arc allows the work to accumulate and integrate properly. We'll discuss what's right for you on your free clarity call.

Is this a substitute for therapy or medical care?

No, and this will never be suggested. Energy healing works beautifully alongside conventional medicine and therapy — addressing dimensions of wellbeing that clinical care doesn't reach. It is complementary, not alternative. Always continue any prescribed treatment.
